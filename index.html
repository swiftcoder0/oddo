Broken Streetlight on Oak Avenue

Resolved
Category
Public Safety
Reported By
Robert Chen
Date Reported
May 15, 2023
Date Resolved
May 20, 2023

Streetlight at the corner of Oak Avenue and 5th Street has been out for over a week. The area becomes completely dark after sunset, creating a safety hazard for pedestrians and increasing risk of accidents. Several residents have reported feeling unsafe walking in this area at night.

Resolution Timeline

May 20, 2023 - 10:30 AM

Resolved

Streetlight repaired by city maintenance crew. Tested and functioning properly.

May 18, 2023 - 2:15 PM

Work Started

Technician arrived on site to assess and repair the streetlight.

May 16, 2023 - 9:00 AM

Assigned to Department

Issue assigned to City Electrical Maintenance Department. Scheduled for repair within 3 business days.

May 15, 2023 - 7:45 PM

Issue Reported

Report submitted through CivicTrack by resident Robert Chen.

Updates & Comments

Robert Chen (Reporter) May 16, 2023

Thank you for the quick response. I noticed the maintenance tag was placed on the pole this morning.

City Maintenance May 18, 2023

Our technician has diagnosed the issue as a faulty photocell. Replacement part has been ordered and will be installed within 48 hours.
